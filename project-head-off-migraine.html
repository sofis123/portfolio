Head Off Migraine

UX Case Study

Project Overview

A UX Case study called "Head Off migraine" to help people with migraines. Migraine is the 3rd most prevalent illness in the world. Based on this subject I tried to develop a project for people who suffer from migraines to understand their triggers and get a better diagnosis. This was created as part of Ironhack UX/UI Design bootcamp.

ROLE

UX Researcher, UX Designer & Interaction Designer

METHODS & TOOLS

Market research, Lean Survey Canvas, Survey, Interviews, Affinity Mapping, User Journey, User flow, Site Map, Wireframes, Moodboard, Competitive Analysis, Moscow, Agile Development,Google Forms, Sketch, Invision, Miro & Sticky Notes

DURATION

1 week UX and 1 week UI

Target Devices

Mobile

Research

I started applying a survey to gather quantitative and qualitative data about user behaviors regarding migraine crisis. I collected 16 responses and interviewed 6 people (patient and neurologist) to find out more about their challenges.

Affinity Mapping

We synthesized our findings with affinity mapping to discover the trends from our user research identifying pain points and define a clear problem to solve.

Personas

Meet Bruna, Our Persona. She's a human resource manager.
Bruna suffers from chronic migraine for more than 15 years, she has to learn how to live with migraine to not interrupt her personal life.

Problem Statement

People with migraine need to find a better way to live with their diagnosis because migraine affects their life constantly.

Mind Map

I created a mind map to analyze the points around the problem and find new ideas to organize my thoughts.

Solution

An app for migraine sufferers to help identify the factors that trigger their crisis. As a MVP users can insert daily information to help themselves identify their triggers. After collecting data the app helps them with a meaningful report that they can share with their neurologist to understand and guide treatments.

User Flow

A user flow shows the path(s) a user can take to complete a task. In this case I focus on the flow where user set their profile and goals and register their daily monitoring

Site Map

A sitemap is an Information Architecture artifact used to show the relationship between pages in a site and to document the organization, navigation, and labeling found in a site.
